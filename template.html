Boat Rental System

The database system that I have designed is for the boat rental center. My job was to design, create, and maintain a database containing information related to boats, owner, captains, reservation Customers, and the Journey. My goal in creating this database is to organize reservation for every customer and any other member of the center who is registered in the database system to send an invoice of their customers and journeys details.

The system calculate the duration between the time that journey started and the time the journey ended And display them in the invoice. Also, calculate the total price and displays it in the invoice. In addition, Shows if boats are available or not. It counts the number of all boats, owners and employees.
